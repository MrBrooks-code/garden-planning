Cross-referenced from the catalog scans. Any species can be added by appending to the data array in the page’s <script>.

All entries below are perennials unless otherwise marked. Spacing values are research-based recommendations from native plant references; soil and sun tolerances reflect typical Wisconsin native-plant guidance.
